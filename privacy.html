Privacy Policy – Astra

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Astra respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data while operating within Discord servers. This policy explains what information is accessed, how it’s used, and how it’s safeguarded.

2. Data Collected

Astra may temporarily access limited data required to perform its functions, including:

  • Messages sent to the bot (for command processing and responses)
  • User ID and username (for identifying user interactions)
  • Server ID (for saving configurations and server-specific settings)

Astra does not store or collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment details, or private messages outside of bot-related interactions.

3. Data Usage

Collected data is used solely for bot functionality, including moderation, automation, and command processing. Temporary logs may be kept for debugging, error resolution, or backup purposes.

4. Data Sharing

Astra does not share, sell, or distribute data to third parties. Data may be visible only to authorized Discord server administrators for management purposes.

5. Data Retention

User data and logs are retained only as long as necessary for Astra’s operation or as required for debugging and stability. Periodic data cleanups are performed to minimize stored information.

6. Security

Astra uses best-effort security measures to protect user data. However, no system can be guaranteed completely secure. Use Astra at your own discretion.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or compliance updates. Continued use of Astra after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
