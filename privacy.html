Privacy Policy – Astra

Effective Date: November 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Astra respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data while running on Discord servers.

2. Data Collected

Astra may temporarily access:

  • Messages you send to the bot (for commands/responses)
  • User ID and username (to identify who is interacting with the bot)
  • Server ID (to store settings or configurations)

Astra does not store sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment info, or private messages outside of command interactions.

3. Data Usage

Data is used to perform bot functions, including moderation, automation, and logging for server management. Logs may be stored temporarily to assist in debugging or backups.

4. Data Sharing

Astra does not share data with third parties. Data may be visible to server administrators for management purposes.

5. Data Retention

User data and logs are kept only as long as necessary for functionality and server operation. Backups may include limited logs for restoration purposes.

6. Security

Astra uses best-effort practices to keep data secure. No system is 100% secure; use the bot at your own discretion.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated as Astra evolves. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically.

8. Contact

Questions about privacy can be directed to: bugzedofficial@gmail.com
